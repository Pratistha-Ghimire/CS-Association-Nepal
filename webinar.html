Educational Webinar 2022

Organized on: July 27 TO AUG 3


Description

Our President, Mr. Anurag Aryal, led the 8-day educational webinar along with our general manager, Mr.Ashish Pandey, and our content creator, Mr.Ankit Gautam, where over 25 students from Kathmandu, Butwal, Chitwan and other different parts of Nepal learned about the fundamentals of Python,Cyber Security, and Big Data for free. One of our objectives is to impart the knowledge we have to other CS enthusiasts at no cost. With this program, we aspired to do the same. Students learned loops, conditionals, function invocation, aliasing, and cloning in the first 5 days dedicated to the Python programming language. In the next 2 days, topics such as Beatle Link, Information Triad, Big Data Hadoop, YARN, HDfS, MapReduce, 3Vs of Big Data, and contemporary ransomware incidents like Wanna Cry and petra Variants were discussed. The last day was reserved for the test based on topics our tutors talked about in the first 7 days. Based on the performance of the students in the exam and engagement throughout the webinar, we provided a certificate of merit to our top 7 students.
