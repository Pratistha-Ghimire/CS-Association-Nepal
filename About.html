OUR MISSION


CS Association of Nepal aspires to build a platform where computer science enthusiasts from all over Nepal, regardless of their background, may meet, learn, and work on research, projects, programs, seminars, and blogs that have real-world significance. Our team would be working on collaborative research using the latest scientific methodologies and data science tools. We also intend to coalesce CS in our society by conducting voluntary programs and classes on different domains of computer science ranging from cyber security to web development and from Machine Learning to data analytics. Our community will hopefully grow in terms of skills, quality and number as we strive to be Nepal's greatest CS hub.
