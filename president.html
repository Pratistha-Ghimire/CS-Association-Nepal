FROM THE PRESIDENT

CS Association of Nepal is a group of Computer Science and Engineering enthusiasts who strive to practically apply the learnt ideas in the real-world. The underlying philosophy of this organization is to create a platform where like- minded people could collaborate on research and various CS-based projects. This diverse society acknowledges the significance of research for the changes we want to see in the world. As we relentlessly attempt to correspond Computer Science in the actual world, we anticipate and appreciate any support along our journey.



-Anurag Aryal
